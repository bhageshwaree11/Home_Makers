About us

Welcome to the community of Home Makers of good manners and lfestyle. The role of home makers celebrates the traditional family dynamics, great home making and shares the beuty of what turns house into home. Home Makers is a destination website and community for those who enjoy traditional family-centred living, and making the best out of their lives at home. Support for the homemaker, good manners, respect for self and others, slow living, and acceptance is at the very heart of our mission. Home Makers is very proud to support a wholesome way of life, among the outspoken modern narrative. Here you will find articles on good old-fashioned recipes, housekeeping, marriage and family values, including tips on how to live a simple vintage-inspired life, free from modern day pressures. We also publish books on the subject of etiquette, daily elegance, and self-esteem with wholesome values in mind.
